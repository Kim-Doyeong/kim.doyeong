Hi! My name is Kim Doyeong and I am a Postdoctoral Appointee at Argonne National Laboratory (ANL). My work focuses primarily on studying the Standard Model of particle physics (SM) and searches for new physics beyond the SM using data collected by the detectors at the Large Hadron Collider (LHC) at European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN).

I enjoy eating tacos and exploring all variety of Maxican food. Recently, I became a happy bunny owner of Bluepepper and San Gu-Leum.
